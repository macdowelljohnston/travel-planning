# San Sebastián 🏖️

**Dates:** 17–20 August 2026 (4 days / 3 nights)
**Base:** Kasa Reina, San Sebastián (Donostia)
**Region:** Basque Country, Spain
**Vibe:** Beaches, pintxos, and coastal hikes.

### Day 1 · Mon 17 Aug · Arrival
- **21:00:** Land in Bilbao (BIO).
- **Bus to San Sebastián:** PESA / Avanza **line E21** from the airport stop. Roughly 75 min to the city centre, €17.10 single. Buses run hourly until **23:45** (extra departures in peak summer, usually at :15 and :45), so a 21:00 landing catches one easily. Aim for the ~22:15 or ~22:45.
- **Fallback (door-to-door, or if the bus is missed):** taxi or shuttle from the airport exit. Shuttle runs about €180 for up to 4, so two shuttles cover a group of 8.
- Check in at **Kasa Reina**.

### Day 2 · Tue 18 Aug · La Concha, Monte Urgull & food tour
- Wake up at leisure.
- Coffee and a walk along the **La Concha promenade**.
- Beach time at **La Concha**.
- Pintxos lunch at **La Cuchara de San Telmo**.
- **14:00:** 1.5-hour round-trip hike up **Monte Urgull**. Grab drinks at the top at **Urgulleko Polboriña**.
- Shower and reset at the hotel.
- **18:30:** Walk to the **Secret Food Tour** meeting point near the **Good Shepherd Cathedral**.
- **19:00:** Secret Food Tour.

### Day 3 · Wed 19 Aug · Tortilla, Monte Igueldo & pintxos hop
- **08:00:** Get to **Bar Antonio**.
- **09:00:** Eat the tortilla at **Bar Antonio**.
- Hike to the top of **Monte Igueldo**. Visit the tower and the amusement park.
- Light lunch in **Gros**.
- Beach time at **Zurriola**.
- Shower and change.
- **20:00:** Pintxos hopping:
  - **Casa Urola**
  - **Ganbara** (Anthony Bourdain's favorite spot)
  - **Lanperna**
  - **La Viña** (Basque cheesecake)

### Day 4 · Thu 20 Aug · Departure
- Check out and leave bags at the hotel.
- Coffee and a walk along the **La Concha promenade**.
- Beach time at **Ondarreta**.
- **18:45:** Leave San Sebastián for Bilbao Airport (pre-booked taxi/transfer for this exact time; if using the public E21 bus instead, take the 18:00 departure, see Notes).
- **20:00:** Arrive at Bilbao Airport.
- **21:40:** Flight departs.

## Notes
- Airport transfer is sorted (see Day 1): last E21 bus is 23:45, so the 21:00 landing is comfortable. Buy bus tickets on the Avanza/PESA site or at the airport.
- **Return leg, read this:** if you use the public E21 bus, it leaves San Sebastián **on the hour** and stops at 21:00, so there is no 18:45 bus. For the 21:40 flight, the **18:00 bus** (arrives ~19:15) gives a safe 2h+ buffer; the **19:00 bus** (arrives ~20:15) is tight at ~1h25. The 18:45 / 20:00 timing in Day 4 only works as a **pre-booked taxi or private transfer**, which is the more comfortable call for the group anyway.
- Book the Secret Food Tour ahead, and reserve any must-do pintxos spots. Mid-August is peak season.
- Thursday's afternoon had a blank note ("XX") after Ondarreta. Add a lunch or last-stop spot if you want to fill it.
