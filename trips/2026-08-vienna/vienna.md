# Vienna 🎻

*One night and a full day in the imperial capital: baroque halls, world-class art, and the best sausage stands on earth.*

**Your window:** Mon 31 Aug from 14:20, all of Tue 1 Sep, until the 22:02 sleeper.
**Base:** Hotel Marc Aurel (1010), in the thick of the old town and the nightlife.
*Recs weighted toward Person 1, with Person 2 and a few of mine folded in.*

> ⚠️ **Heads up: Vienna runs on cash.** Loads of the best spots (Würstelstände, older bars, small Beisl) are cash-only. Pull out euros before you start.

---

### Day 1 · Mon 31 Aug · Arrive & first night
> You drop your bags in the 1st district and the whole old town is on your doorstep. A cathedral, a coffee, a sausage, then the Bermuda Triangle, which happens to be your street.

- 👀 **Stephansdom**, 5 minutes from the hotel. Vienna's great Gothic cathedral. Climb the south tower if the legs are willing.
- 👀 **Peterskirche**, a jewel-box Baroque church two minutes on. Free, with a free organ recital most late afternoons.
- ☕ **Coffee and cake, Viennese-style.** A proper Kaffeehaus: **Café Central** (grand and columned) or **Café Sacher** for the original Sachertorte.
- 🌭 **Your first Würstelstand.** Person 1 ate their favourite three times and said it changed their life ([their pin](https://www.google.com/maps/place/W%C3%BCrstelstand/@48.2114965,16.3773391,20.86z/data=!4m19!1m12!4m11!1m6!1m2!1s0x476d07bbd806e0e5:0x5fa5f935f7f0ca57!2sSigmund+Freud+Museum,+Berggasse,+Vienna,+Austria!2m2!1d16.3630759!2d48.2186272!1m3!2m2!1d16.3771171!2d48.2113613!3m5!1s0x476d07a0510894b1:0x2b0e892b270738ec!8m2!3d48.2115422!4d16.3775781)). Get a Käsekrainer (cheese-filled) with mustard and a crusty roll. Not your last.
- 🍴 **Dinner.** The Viennese classic: a schnitzel bigger than the plate at **Figlmüller**, or traditional Beisl fare with a garden at **Glacis Beisl** in the MuseumsQuartier.
- 🍸 **The night out.** You're staying inside the **Bermuda Triangle**, Vienna's bar cluster by Schwedenplatz. Person 1's top call: **Travel Shack** ("you are going to die at this place, it's awesome"). Round it out with **Blue Mustard** (slick cocktails) and the **Leo Hillinger** wine bar.

> ⭐ **The Move:** Travel Shack and the Bermuda Triangle, a 3-minute stagger from your bed. Person 1's number-one Vienna call, and it's your actual street.

> ⚠️ **Heads up:** the big clubs (**Volksgarten**, "best music ever" per Person 1, and **Prater Dome**) mostly run Thursday to Saturday, so on a Monday they're likely dark. Tonight is a bar night; save the clubs for a weekend return.

### Day 2 · Tue 1 Sep · Art, sausages & the sleeper
> Your one full day. Hit a world-class museum or two, graze the sausage stands like Person 1 ordered, and wander the Baroque core before the night train.

- ☕ **Brunch.** Slow start at **Erich** (cool café in the 2nd district) or another Kaffeehaus.
- 🖼️ **The flagship: Kunsthistorisches Museum (KHM).** One of the world's great art museums (Bruegel, Vermeer, Caravaggio) in a building as jaw-dropping as the paintings. Give it 2 to 3 hours and take coffee under the cupola.
- 🖼️ **A second, if you want one (Person 1's picks):** the **Albertina** (Monet to Picasso, plus a terrace over the Opera) or the **Leopold** (the world's greatest Schiele collection, and Klimt) in the MuseumsQuartier.
- 🌭 **Lunch on your feet.** More Würstelstände, per orders. **Bitzinger am Albertinaplatz** (behind the Opera, iconic, they'll pour you a sekt with your sausage) sits right by the Albertina. Or a **kebab stand**, also Person 1-approved.
- 👀 **Baroque Vienna.** The **Austrian National Library State Hall (Prunksaal)**, one of the most beautiful rooms on the continent, then the **Hofburg** grounds and a wander through the **Naschmarkt**.
- 👀 **If time:** the **Belvedere** for Klimt's "The Kiss."
- 🚕 **Bags and out.** Collect luggage from Hotel Marc Aurel, early dinner (**Mochi**, superb Japanese, book ahead, or back to Glacis Beisl), then **U1 Schwedenplatz → Hauptbahnhof** for the **22:02 Nightjet**.

> ⭐ **The Move:** an hour lost in the KHM, then grazing Würstelstände across the 1st district. High and low, the way Vienna is meant to be eaten.

> ⚠️ **Skip the Sigmund Freud Museum.** Both your friends and the reviews agree: it covers his flat and upbringing, not the ideas. Not worth the time on a one-day visit.

## 🗺️ Vienna cheat sheet
Everything your friends flagged, plus a few of mine, by category.

**🌭 Eat (do this a lot)**
- **Würstelstände (sausage stands)** are the whole point. Bitzinger am Albertinaplatz (iconic), Leo's (said to be the oldest), and [Person 1's life-changing pin](https://www.google.com/maps/place/W%C3%BCrstelstand/@48.2114965,16.3773391,20.86z/data=!4m19!1m12!4m11!1m6!1m2!1s0x476d07bbd806e0e5:0x5fa5f935f7f0ca57!2sSigmund+Freud+Museum,+Berggasse,+Vienna,+Austria!2m2!1d16.3630759!2d48.2186272!1m3!2m2!1d16.3771171!2d48.2113613!3m5!1s0x476d07a0510894b1:0x2b0e892b270738ec!8m2!3d48.2115422!4d16.3775781). Order the Käsekrainer.
- **Kebab stands:** everywhere, all great. Person 1's orders: eat at every one.
- **Schnitzel:** Figlmüller (the giant classic).
- **Sit-down:** Glacis Beisl (traditional, garden), Mochi (Japanese, book ahead), or Plachutta (Tafelspitz, the Viennese boiled-beef institution) for one nice dinner.
- **Café / brunch:** Erich, Café Central, Café Sacher (Sachertorte).

**🍸 Drink & go out**
- **Bars:** Travel Shack (Person 1's #1), Blue Mustard, Leo Hillinger wine bar. All in or beside the Bermuda Triangle by your hotel.
- **Clubs:** Volksgarten (21+, "best music ever") and Prater Dome. Weekend-only, so most likely shut on your Monday.

**🖼️ Museums (Person 1)**
- Kunsthistorisches Museum (KHM), Albertina, Leopold. Pick one or two, not all three.
- 🚫 Skip the Sigmund Freud Museum.

**👀 See**
- Stephansdom, Peterskirche, Hofburg, the Austrian National Library State Hall, Naschmarkt, and the Belvedere (Klimt). The State Opera sells very cheap standing tickets if a performance is on (check the schedule for 1 Sep).

**💶 Cash:** bring plenty. Many of the best places are cash-only.
