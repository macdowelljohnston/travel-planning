### Day 1 · Mon 31 Aug · Budapest → Vienna
> A short hop north, then a first evening in the imperial capital with nowhere to rush to.

- 🚆 **EC 64 to Vienna, arriving 14:20.** Departs **Budapest-Kelenföld 11:55** (the train also calls at **Budapest-Keleti ~11:40**, more central, so board wherever suits). Arrives **Wien Hbf 14:20**, 2nd class, roughly 2h25 to 2h40. This lands you right at hotel check-in with the whole evening free.
- 🚕 **To the hotel:** from Wien Hauptbahnhof take the **U1 metro straight to Schwedenplatz** (no changes), then a 3-minute walk. Around 15 minutes door to door.
- 🛏️ **Check into Hotel Marc Aurel**, Marc-Aurel-Straße 8, 1010 Wien. A quiet street in the 1st district, steps from Schwedenplatz and the Danube canal, and a 5-minute stroll to Stephansdom. Drop the bags. (Booked, $120.)
- 🌭 **Afternoon and evening in Vienna:** Stephansdom on your doorstep, a Kaffeehaus, your first Würstelstand, and a night in the Bermuda Triangle bar cluster around your hotel.

> 💡 **The full Vienna plan** (day-by-day, plus every bar, sausage stand, and museum your friends flagged) lives in the **City Guide** tab.

### Day 2 · Tue 1 Sep · Vienna, then the night train
> A full day in one of Europe's most beautiful cities, capped by boarding the sleeper to Berlin.

- 🖼️ **A full Vienna day:** a world-class museum (KHM, Albertina, or Leopold), the Baroque core, and as many Würstelstände as you can manage. Full plan and rec list in the **City Guide** tab.
- 🚕 **Luggage:** check out in the morning and leave your bags at Hotel Marc Aurel for the day (central, easy to swing back for). Early dinner, then the **U1 from Schwedenplatz direct to Hauptbahnhof** for the sleeper.
- 🚆 **22:10: Nightjet NJ 456, Wien Hbf → Berlin Hbf, arriving 07:46.** Your bed is a **berth in a 6-bunk couchette** (coach 41, berth 311, lower bunk). **Breakfast is included.**
- 🛏️ **Sleep on rails.** Settle in, grab a nightcap in the couchette, and let the train do the work.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the night train. It replaces both a travel day and a hotel night, and you wake up rolling into Berlin.

> 💡 **Where next:** you arrive Berlin Hbf at 07:46 on 2 Sep. The Berlin chapter is its own trip, starting the moment you step off the train.
