# 🇨🇿 PRAGUE

**Where to stay:** Prague 1 — Old Town (Staré Město) or New Town (Nové Město) near Wenceslas Square / Náměstí Republiky. Everything walkable, roll home from the bars.

### Day 1 — Thu 27 Aug — Arrival & ease-in night
> Everyone's been traveling. Tonight is warm-up; the big Prague night is tomorrow.

- **~10:00–11:30** — Land, bags, pre-booked minivan to the Airbnb (~30–40 min). Bags may beat check-in — arrange a luggage drop.
- **Lunch (first beers):** classic Czech beer hall. **Lokál Dlouhááá** (fresh tank Pilsner Urquell, great cheap Czech food) is the move. *U Fleků* is the touristy-but-fun brewery alternative.
- **Afternoon (easy orientation):** stroll Old Town Square → Astronomical Clock → Charles Bridge. Then decompress at a **beer garden** — **Riegrovy Sady** (lively, local, big group friendly) or **Letná** (postcard views over the river & city).
- **Evening (ease-in crawl):** dinner, then a few Old Town bars. Good picks: **Hemingway Bar** (top cocktails), **Vzorkovna / "Dog Bar"** (wild cheap underground, very lads), **Anonymous Bar**. Keep it to ~1–2am — pace yourselves.

### Day 2 — Fri 28 Aug — Full day + the BIG night (all-nighter → train)
> Save some energy in the day; tonight runs till the 06:01.

- **Morning/midday (sightseeing + views):** Prague Castle + **Malá Strana** (Lesser Town), walk back over Charles Bridge. Or a relaxed morning if bodies need it.
- **Shopping:** Na Příkopě & Pařížská (Pařížská = luxury flex), or design/gift shops around Old Town.
- **Afternoon (beer garden reset):** Letná or Riegrovy Sady again — hydrate-ish, big group energy, sunset views. Optional lads add-on: **shooting range** or **beer-bike** (both very popular here) if you want one adrenaline hit.
- **Early dinner + pre-drinks** at the Airbnb (~7–8pm) — fuel up, you're going long.
- **The main event (crawl → club):**
  - Old Town bar crawl → then a proper club. Big-night options: **Karlovy Lázně** (5-floor mega-club, iconic lads-trip spot), **Lucerna Music Bar** (legendary 80s/90s video party — check the night's schedule), **Roxy**, **Chapeau Rouge** (multi-floor party bar).
  - **Reserve a table** at your chosen club for a group of 8.
- **~04:30–05:00** — Peel off, grab bags from the Airbnb, walk/Bolt to **Praha hl.n.** (main station, central). Grab food/water.
- **06:01** — Board. Sleep the 6h57 to Budapest. 😴

# 🇭🇺 BUDAPEST

**Where to stay:** **District VII (Erzsébetváros)** — the Jewish Quarter, home of the ruin bars. Roll home from the party. (District VI near Andrássy also fine.)

### Day 3 — Sat 29 Aug — Arrival + big Saturday night
> You'll land wrecked from the all-nighter. Afternoon = full recovery, then Budapest's best night.

- **12:58** — Arrive Budapest-Keleti. Bolt to Airbnb (~10 min), check in, drop bags.
- **Recovery lunch** (District VII is packed with great spots) → then **thermal baths to reset the bodies**: a **Széchenyi** day soak (grand, in City Park) or **Rudas** (rooftop hot tub with Danube views). Nap back at the Airbnb.
- **Saturday night — your peak window. Two ways to play it (pick one):**
  - **A) Ruin-bar crawl (recommended given the all-nighter):** self-paced, forgiving. **Szimpla Kert** (the original & most famous) → **Ellátó Kert / Mazel Tov** → finish at **Instant-Fogas** (giant multi-room club complex — perfect for a group). Reserve a spot at Instant-Fogas.
  - **B) Széchenyi "Sparty":** the iconic Saturday-night pool party. Huge, unforgettable — but it's a ticketed, high-energy commitment on a low-sleep day. **Book ahead if you choose it** (it's the *only* night it runs).
  - 👉 *Trade-off:* Sparty is Saturday-only, so choosing the ruin-bar crawl means skipping Sparty this trip.

### Day 4 — Sun 30 Aug — See Budapest + final night
> The recovered day. This is your sightseeing/shopping/food showcase.

- **Morning:** proper **thermal baths** if you didn't Saturday (Széchenyi is the grand experience).
- **Midday (the classic views):** Buda side — **Buda Castle**, **Fisherman's Bastion**, **Gellért Hill** lookout. Cross the **Chain Bridge**.
- **Shopping + food:** **Great Market Hall** (Central Market — souvenirs, paprika, langos), **Váci utca**, **Andrássy Avenue**.
- **Early evening:** a **Danube sunset river cruise** — cheap, stunning, great group thing as the Parliament lights come up.
- **Final night (mellower — Monday travel):** group dinner → last ruin-bar session (Szimpla runs daily). Keep it a notch lighter than Saturday.

### Day 5 — Mon 31 Aug — Departure
- **Morning:** brunch, pack, check out.
- **Afternoon:** pre-booked minivan to **BUD** (~30–40 min; arrive with buffer) → fly out.
