# 🇨🇿 PRAGUE

**Where to stay:** Prague 1. Old Town (Staré Město) or New Town (Nové Město) near Wenceslas Square / Náměstí Republiky. Everything walkable, roll home from the bars.

### Day 1 · Thu 27 Aug · Arrival & ease-in night
> You land, drop the bags, and let Prague introduce itself gently. Tonight is a warm-up. The big one is tomorrow, and it does not end until the train.

- **~10:00–11:30** 🚕 **Land and settle.** Bags, then a pre-booked minivan to the Airbnb (~30–40 min). Bags may beat check-in, so arrange a luggage drop.
- 🍺 **Lunch, first beers: Lokál Dlouhááá.** Tank-fresh Pilsner Urquell poured properly and honest Czech cooking at honest prices. (U Fleků is the touristy-but-fun brewery alternative.)
- 🚲 **Beer bike, orientation on wheels.** A 1.5–2hr pedal-powered bar that doubles as your city intro and a warm-up, low-key enough not to wreck the ease-in day. Book ahead for 8; summer weekends sell out.
  - Alt if you would rather stay off the booze early: stroll Old Town Square → Astronomical Clock → Charles Bridge, then a beer garden (Riegrovy Sady or Letná for the views).
- 🍸 **Evening ease-in crawl.** Dinner, then a few Old Town bars: Hemingway Bar (serious cocktails), Vzorkovna / "Dog Bar" (wild, cheap, underground), Anonymous Bar. Keep it to ~1–2am and pace yourselves.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the beer bike. Eight lads, a rolling bar, and the whole Old Town for a backdrop. It sets the tone for the trip.

> ⚠️ **Heads up:** Prague's gentlemen's clubs (Goldfingers, Darling Cabaret) are an option tonight, but bill scams are common. Read the safety note at the bottom before anyone goes.

### Day 2 · Fri 28 Aug · The big one (all-nighter → train)
> The headline day. A relaxed afternoon, then a full send that never touches a bed. You party until dawn and walk straight onto the 06:01.

- 👀 **Morning and midday sights.** Prague Castle and **Malá Strana** (Lesser Town), back over Charles Bridge. Or a slow morning if bodies need it.
- 🛍️ **Shopping.** Na Příkopě and Pařížská (the luxury flex), or design and gift shops around Old Town.
- 🍺 **Afternoon beer-garden reset.** Letná or Riegrovy Sady, sunset over the city and river, big group energy. Keep it to fuel and pace, not send-it. The beer bike was yesterday by design.
- 🍴 **Early dinner and pre-drinks** at the Airbnb (~7–8pm). Line the stomach. You are going long.
- 🍸 **The main event.** Old Town crawl into a proper club: Karlovy Lázně (5 floors, iconic), Lucerna Music Bar (legendary 80s/90s video party; check the schedule), Roxy, or Chapeau Rouge.
- **~04:30–05:00** 🚕 **Peel off.** Grab the bags, walk or Bolt to **Praha hl.n.** (main station, central). Grab food and water.
- **06:01** 🚆 **Board and sleep** the 6h57 to Budapest.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the all-nighter itself. Turning the brutal 06:01 into a badge of honour instead of an alarm-clock disaster is the whole trip in miniature.

> 🎯 **Book it:** reserve a table at your chosen club for the group of 8.

# 🇭🇺 BUDAPEST

**Where to stay:** **District VII (Erzsébetváros)**, the Jewish Quarter, home of the ruin bars. Roll home from the party. (District VI near Andrássy also works.)

### Day 3 · Sat 29 Aug · Arrival + big Saturday night
> You arrive wrecked and let Budapest heal you. Thermal water in the afternoon, a nap, then the city's best night once you have some sleep in you.

- **12:58** 🚆 **Arrive Budapest-Keleti.** Bolt to the Airbnb (~10 min), check in, drop bags.
- 🍴 **Recovery lunch** in District VII, which is wall-to-wall with good spots.
- ♨️ **Thermal baths to resurrect the bodies.** A **Széchenyi** day soak (grand, neo-baroque, in City Park) or **Rudas** (a rooftop hot tub looking over the Danube). Nap after.
- 🍸 **Saturday night, your peak window. Two ways to play it (pick one):**
  1. **Ruin-bar crawl (recommended after the all-nighter).** Self-paced and forgiving: **Szimpla Kert** (the original and most famous) → **Ellátó Kert / Mazel Tov** → finish at **Instant-Fogas** (a giant multi-room club complex built for groups).
  2. **Széchenyi "Sparty".** The iconic Saturday-only thermal pool party. Huge and unforgettable, but a ticketed, high-energy commitment on low sleep. Book ahead if you choose it.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the baths. Sinking into 38°C water after a night train is the single best feeling of the whole trip.

> 💡 **Local intel:** Sparty runs Saturdays only, so choosing the ruin-bar crawl means skipping it this trip. Decide before you book.

### Day 4 · Sun 30 Aug · See Budapest + final night
> The recovered day, and your chance to actually see this stunning city. Views, market, a river cruise, and a softer last night before the flight home.

- ♨️ **Morning baths** if you skipped Saturday. Széchenyi is the grand experience.
- 👀 **Midday, the classic views.** Buda side: **Buda Castle**, **Fisherman's Bastion**, the **Gellért Hill** lookout. Cross the **Chain Bridge** on foot.
- 🛍️ **Shopping and food.** **Great Market Hall** (paprika, langos, souvenirs under a cathedral of a roof), **Váci utca**, **Andrássy Avenue**.
- 🚢 **Early evening: Danube sunset cruise.** Cheap, stunning, and the Parliament lighting up over the water is the picture you take home.
- 🍸 **Final night (mellower, Monday travel).** Group dinner, then a last ruin-bar session at Szimpla. A notch lighter than Saturday.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the sunset cruise. Twenty euros for the best skyline in Europe, glass in hand.

### Day 5 · Mon 31 Aug · Departure
- 🛏️ **Morning.** Brunch, pack, check out.
- 🚕 **Afternoon.** Pre-booked minivan to **BUD** (~30–40 min; arrive with buffer), then fly out.

## ⚠️ Adult-scene safety note (read before you go)
Prague's adult venues are legal and mainstream, but tourist **bill scams are common**. Protect yourselves:
- **Agree every price up front:** entry, each drink, and any "private" anything. Get it in writing or on the menu.
- **Never run a tab or hand over a card to hold.** Pay per round, in cash, and keep the receipt.
- **One inflated "champagne" can cost hundreds of €.** If a hostess pressures bottles, decline clearly.
- **Ignore street promoters and "free entry" flyers.** Those are the classic scam funnels. Go to a known-name venue directly.
- **Buddy system plus a sober-ish anchor.** Nobody wanders off alone; agree a spend cap before entering.
- Same rules apply in Budapest. If in doubt, ruin bars are the safer party bet.
