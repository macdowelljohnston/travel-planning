# Budget: Prague + Budapest Lads Trip

**Tier:** Mid-range · **Group:** 8 · **Nights:** 4 (2 Prague, 2 Budapest)
**Currency:** USD estimates (Prague = CZK, Budapest = HUF on the ground)
_Rough per-person estimates excluding international flights. Verify closer to the date._

## Per person, whole trip

| Category | Low | High | Notes |
|----------|-----|------|-------|
| Airbnb (2 cities, split ÷8) | $180 | $320 | Central apartments; ~$90–160/night/person total split |
| Train Prague→Budapest | $30 | $45 | EuroCity + seat reservation |
| Airport transfers (both ends) | $20 | $40 | Minivans split ÷8 |
| Food (4 days) | $120 | $220 | Beer halls, ruin bars, market food, group dinners |
| Nightlife (covers, drinks, tables) | $200 | $400 | The big variable; table service pushes this up |
| Baths / Sparty / cruise / activities | $60 | $130 | Széchenyi ~$25–35; Sparty ~$60; cruise ~$20 |
| Shopping / misc | $50 | $150 | Personal |
| **Per-person total** | **~$660** | **~$1,305** | ≈ $165–325/day |

## Group total (×8)
- **Low:** ~$5,300  ·  **High:** ~$10,400

## Cost levers
- **Biggest swing = nightlife.** Bottle service/tables can double a night. Decide per city where it's worth it.
- One big Airbnb splitting ÷8 is where mid-range gets its value; it keeps lodging cheap while everyone's together.
- Book train + Airbnb early = meaningfully cheaper for 8 in late August.
