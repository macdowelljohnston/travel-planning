### Day 1 · Wed 2 Sep · Arrival, a solo morning & the reunion
> You roll into Berlin over breakfast with the whole city to yourself for a few hours. The trick is turning the gap before your friend lands into the best quiet morning of the trip.

- 🚆 **07:46: arrive Berlin Hauptbahnhof**, rested and fed off the sleeper. This is where Berlin begins.
- 🛏️ **Drop the bag first.** It's about 10 minutes to the **Garner Hotel on Gendarmenmarkt** (U/S-Bahn or a short taxi). Check-in isn't until ~15:00, but they'll hold your luggage, so ditch it and go explore hands-free.
- ☕ **Coffee on Gendarmenmarkt.** Berlin's most elegant square is your doorstep: the Konzerthaus flanked by the French and German cathedrals. Ease into the day.
- 🚶 **The solo icons walk (roughly 9:00 to 11:30).** Everything is walkable from the hotel and blissfully empty this early:
  - 👀 **Bebelplatz** and **Unter den Linden**, the grand boulevard.
  - 👀 **Brandenburg Gate**, best seen before the tour groups arrive.
  - 👀 **Memorial to the Murdered Jews of Europe**, two minutes on. Sobering and quiet in the morning.
  - 👀 **Reichstag** and the government quarter. If you want to go up the glass dome, it's free but needs advance online registration (see Book it).
  - 👀 Loop back east toward **Museum Island** and the **Berlin Cathedral** if you have time.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the Brandenburg Gate at ~9am, near-empty. You get the money shot to yourself, which almost never happens later in the day.

> 🎯 **Book it:** if you want the **Reichstag dome** (great free views), register online in advance. Walk-ups are not allowed.

- ✈️ **The reunion.** Your friend lands at **BER around 10:50**. Fastest route in: the **FEX Airport Express to Hauptbahnhof** (~30 min) or the S9 / S45. Realistically she's in the centre by **~12:00 to 12:15**.
- 📍 **Meet up around 12:15 to 12:30.** Easiest rendezvous is **the hotel on Gendarmenmarkt** (she can drop her bag too), or the **Brandenburg Gate** if you are still out roaming.
- 🍴 **Lunch together** around Gendarmenmarkt or Hackescher Markt. First proper catch-up over food.
- 🕒 **~15:00: check in at the Garner**, freshen up. You have been on a night train and she has been on a flight, so a shower and a beat helps before the evening.
- 🌆 **Wednesday evening in Berlin:** to be planned together.

> 💡 **Local intel:** the 3pm check-in is the standard assumption. Worth a quick message to the Garner to confirm, and to ask about early check-in in case a room is ready when you meet up.
