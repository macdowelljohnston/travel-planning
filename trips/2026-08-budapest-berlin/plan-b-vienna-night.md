### Day 1 · Mon 31 Aug · Budapest → Vienna
> A short hop north, then a first evening in the imperial capital with nowhere to rush to.

- 🚆 **Midday: railjet, Budapest-Keleti → Wien Hauptbahnhof.** Around 2h20, roughly hourly. Book an ÖBB **Sparschiene** fare.
- 🛏️ **Check in** somewhere central (around the Innere Stadt or near the Ring).
- 🍸 **First evening in Vienna.** A stroll past a floodlit **Stephansdom**, then dinner and a glowing glass of Grüner Veltliner. Ease into it.

> ⭐ **The Move:** an unhurried first night. This is the whole reason to choose Plan B: Vienna without a train to catch.

### Day 2 · Tue 1 Sep · Vienna, then the night train
> A full day in one of Europe's most beautiful cities, capped by boarding the sleeper to Berlin.

- ☕ **Slow morning.** Coffee and cake at **Café Central**, the grand old literary café.
- 👀 **The big hitters.** Pick one palace and do it well: **Schönbrunn** (gardens and Gloriette views) or the **Belvedere** (Klimt's "The Kiss"). Then wander the **Ringstrasse** and the **Hofburg**.
- 🍴 **Lunch at the Naschmarkt**, Vienna's sprawling market, or a schnitzel at **Figlmüller**.
- 🛍️ **Afternoon** at leisure: the **MuseumsQuartier** courtyards, or just a long walk and one more Kaffeehaus.
- 🚕 **Collect the bags**, early dinner near the station.
- 🚆 **22:02: Nightjet NJ456, Wien Hbf → Berlin Hbf.** Roughly 9.5 hours, daily.
- 🛏️ **Book a sleeper cabin** (from ~€89): real bed, breakfast, arrive rested.

> 🎯 **Book it:** both the Vienna hotel and the Nightjet sleeper. Sleepers sell out first, so lock the cabin early.
