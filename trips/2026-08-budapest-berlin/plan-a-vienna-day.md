### Day 1 · Mon 31 Aug · Budapest → Vienna, then the night train
> A fast morning hop to Vienna, an easy afternoon in the old imperial capital, and then you board the sleeper as the city lights come on.

- 🚆 **Late morning: railjet, Budapest-Keleti → Wien Hauptbahnhof.** Around 2h20, comfortable and roughly hourly. Book an ÖBB **Sparschiene** advance fare for the cheapest seat.
- 🚕 **Stash the bags** in the left-luggage lockers at Wien Hbf so you can roam hands-free.
- 👀 **Afternoon in Vienna.** A loop of the grand **Ringstrasse** and a look inside **Stephansdom**, then the essential Viennese ritual: coffee and Sachertorte in a proper **Kaffeehaus** (Café Central or Café Sacher).
- 🍴 **Early dinner** near the centre. Wiener schnitzel at **Figlmüller** if you want the classic, or a plate at the **Naschmarkt** stalls.
- 🚆 **22:02: Nightjet NJ456, Wien Hbf → Berlin Hbf.** Roughly 9.5 hours, daily.
- 🛏️ **Sleep on rails.** Book a **sleeper cabin** (from ~€89), not a seat: real bed, breakfast in the morning, and you arrive rested.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the sleeper itself. It swallows the entire Vienna → Berlin journey into one night and saves you a hotel, which is why this plan is so lean.

> 🎯 **Book it:** Nightjet private sleepers sell out first. Lock the cabin the moment the date is fixed.
