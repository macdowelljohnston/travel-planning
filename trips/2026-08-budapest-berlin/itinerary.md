## The travel plan: Budapest → Vienna → Berlin

### Leg 1 · Mon 31 Aug · Budapest → Vienna 🚆
> A clean, fast start. Two hours and change through the Hungarian and Austrian countryside, and you are in Vienna in time for lunch.

- 🚆 **Railjet, Budapest-Keleti → Wien Hauptbahnhof.** Around 2h20, comfortable and frequent (roughly hourly). Book an ÖBB **Sparschiene** advance fare for the cheapest seats.
- 🚕 **Drop the bags.** Left-luggage lockers at Wien Hbf so you can roam Vienna hands-free.

### Vienna · Mon 31 Aug · A few unhurried hours 👀
> Not a full sightseeing campaign, just enough to say you were here: a grand boulevard, a cathedral, and a proper Viennese coffee.

- 👀 A loop of the **Ringstrasse** and **Stephansdom**, then coffee and cake at a classic **Kaffeehaus** (Café Central or Café Sacher).
- 🍴 Early dinner near the centre before the night train.

### Leg 2 · Mon 31 Aug (night) · Vienna → Berlin, ÖBB Nightjet 🚆
> The best part. You board in Vienna, have a nightcap as the city slides away, and wake up pulling into Berlin.

- 🚆 **Nightjet NJ456: Wien Hbf 22:02 → Berlin Hbf 07:33.** Runs daily, roughly 9.5 hours.
- 🛏️ **Book a sleeper**, not a seat. Rough fares: seat from €29, couchette from €49, private sleeper from €89. The sleeper includes breakfast and a real lie-down.

> ⭐ **The Move:** the sleeper cabin. It replaces both a travel day and a hotel night, and arriving in a city by night train beats any airport transfer.

> 🎯 **Book it:** Nightjet sleepers sell out, especially the private cabins. Book as soon as your date is fixed.

## Choosing the shape (let's lock this)
1. **Vienna by afternoon + night train (this draft).** Home ~3 Sep. Leanest, only 2 hotel nights, one night on the sleeper.
2. **A full night in Vienna + night train.** Adds a Vienna hotel night on 31 Aug, night train on 1 Sep, Berlin 2–3 Sep, home ~4 Sep.
3. **All daytime trains (real bed nightly).** Vienna overnight, then the daytime Vienna → Berlin train. Note: that daytime route is disrupted through 2026 (diverted via Munich, 8h+), so it is the slowest option.
